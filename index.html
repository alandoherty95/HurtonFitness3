Fitness Bootcamp

Iain runs outdoor bootcamps for all levels of fitness. Reach your fitness goals in a supportive environment. Exercising with others is great motivation to push your limits. New joiners are always welcome. The bootcamps consist of circuit training with various stations including kettlebells, dumbbells, medicine balls, battle ropes and push up boards.

About Iain

I love all kinds of exercise including swimming, lifting weights, running and hiking. Fitness is my passion in life. I found my love of fitness at 16 years old and I haven't looked back since. Through my love of fitness, I hope to inspire as many people as possible to live a healthy lifestyle.

My favourite way to exercise would be a push/pull training plan. Pull exercises focuses on back and arm while other exercises cover legs and core. That being said, I really enjoy circuit training because it is challenging and makes me want to better my last attempt.
